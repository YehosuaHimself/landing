01 — The Artist

Since 1st January 2025, YEHOSUA HIMSELF has been walking across Europe and the Alps — backpack and DJ mixer in tow — performing in 67+ venues across 15+ countries, covering more than 10,000 km on foot, and still moving.

A psytechno and psytrance artist on SGD Recordings, Yehosua occupies a singular space: the intersection of physical endurance, sonic ritual, and spiritual inquiry. From 50 souls at coastal dunes at sunrise in Iberia to thousands at festival main stages in Belgium — his sets are not entertainment. They are threshold experiences, designed to move bodies and dissolve boundaries simultaneously.

The 30th ADE is not merely a festival milestone. It is a moment that calls for artists whose work embodies the philosophy of movement, transformation, and electronic music as a living practice — not just a career.

02 — Why ADE 2026

ADE's 30th anniversary demands acts that are symbolically complete. A man who has walked 10,000 km across Europe with a DJ mixer — arriving in Amsterdam in October 2026 — is not just a booking. It is a moment. If not a movement.

Yehosua fits three distinct ADE pillars simultaneously: as a working psytechno artist for the Festival; as a concept artist for Arts & Culture; and as a practitioner-builder and manual-digital creator — for the Pro Conference.

He is not pitching a performance. He is offering a narrative the 30th edition can own.
